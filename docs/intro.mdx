Each platform supports the following content types:
- **Linear TV (incl. FAST)** — Ad insertion for live linear TV channels and FAST streams
- **VOD (Video On Demand)** — Pre-roll, mid-roll, and post-roll ads
- **Advanced Ad Formats** — Inline and interstitial ads
